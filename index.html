The Treble Clef

Used for the Right Hand. It spirals around the 2nd line from the bottom: the G line.

💡 Tip: Spaces spell F - A - C - E. It's the easiest way to find your place!

The Bass Clef

Used for the Left Hand. The two dots surround the 4th line from the bottom: the F line.

💡 Tip: All Cows Eat Grass (Spaces).

Visualizing the Scale

Musical notes move in a repeating cycle: A - B - C - D - E - F - G. After G, it goes back to A.

  • Step: Line to Space (C to D)
  • Skip: Line to Line (C to E)
