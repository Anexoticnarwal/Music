The Grand Staff

Pianists read the Grand Staff. The top staff is the Treble Clef (usually right hand), and the bottom is the Bass Clef (usually left hand).

Treble Clef (G-Clef)

The symbol curls around the 2nd line, which is G.

Lines: Every Good Boy Does Fine
Spaces: F - A - C - E
Bass Clef (F-Clef)

The two dots surround the 4th line, which is F.

Lines: Good Boys Do Fine Always
Spaces: All Cows Eat Grass

Middle C: The Anchor

Middle C doesn't live on either staff—it lives on its own tiny line (a ledger line) right in the middle of the two. Use it as your home base.
